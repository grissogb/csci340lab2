Dogs vs Cats

It's very simple. You can see here are two images, of a dog and a cat, as well as two buttons, labeled as such. Your job, should you choose to accept it, is to choose which of the animals in the image is better.

What "better" means is, of course, up to you. It could mean cuter, it could mean more likely to win in a fight, it could be who's gonna win a basketball competition (although the dogs have the advantage there, I'd say).

When you've made your decision, click the loser's button, and it will change to a new one of the same animal, and in the bar next to this button you will see the number increase, as this animal's "winning streak" as it were.

Ultimately, this site is for you to have fun, and see some pictures of cute animals.
